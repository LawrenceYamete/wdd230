Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Montserrat easily define the words or the title. It is old posters and signs in the traditional Montserrat neighborhood of Buenos Aires inspired Julieta Ulanovsky to design this typeface and rescue the beauty of urban typography that emerged in the first half of the twentieth century.

Paragraph Font: Quicksand

Quicksand is a display sans serif with rounded terminals. Clear and easy to read words for heading. It is designed for display purposes but kept legible enough to use in small sizes as well.
